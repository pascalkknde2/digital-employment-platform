# 🎯 2. What is the project about?

### Project Title:

**Digital Employment Support (KMCAS-164-007)**

### Core Objective:

Help **economically inactive people** gain **digital skills** so they can:

* Enter the workforce
* Consider remote/hybrid jobs
* Become digitally included in society

👉 In simple terms:

> This is a **digital inclusion + employability program**

# 🧩 4. Project structure (VERY important)

The contract is split into **3 LOTS**:

---

## 🧑‍💻 LOT 1a & 1b – Digital Skills for Inactive People

### Target users:

* Unemployed
* Economically inactive individuals
* Digitally excluded people

### Services delivered:

* Up to **40 hours training per participant**
* Basic digital literacy:

  * Email
  * Word / Excel
  * Teams / Skype
* Online safety
* Creating:

  * Email accounts
  * LinkedIn profiles
  * Local digital accounts (My Kirklees)

### Outcome:

* Job readiness
* Confidence with digital tools
* Access to employment opportunities

👉 **Business interpretation:**
This is a **“Digital Bootcamp for Beginners”**

---

## 🧠 LOT 2 – Neurodiversity Digital Support

### Target:

* Employees (neurodivergent individuals)
* Employers / managers

### Focus:

Using **technology + digital content** to:

* Support neurodivergent workers
* Improve workplace inclusion

### Deliverables:

* Digital resources (web, social media, podcasts)
* Assistive tech guidance
* Employer training on:

  * Inclusion
  * Reasonable adjustments
  * Retention strategies

👉 **Business interpretation:**
This is a **“Digital Inclusion + Workplace Transformation Program”**
